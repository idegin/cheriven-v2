Nigeria's young people are brimming with talent — what many lack is opportunity. Our Youth Skills & Employability programme bridges that gap with certified, market-relevant vocational training and real entrepreneurship support.

This session equipped participants with practical, in-demand skills, startup toolkits and mentorship to help them take their first steps toward self-reliance. Graduates leave not only with a certificate, but with a plan and the resources to act on it.

From skills to self-reliance — that is how lasting change is built, one young person at a time.
