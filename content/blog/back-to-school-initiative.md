Education is the surest path out of poverty — but for too many Nigerian children, the cost of books, uniforms and fees puts school out of reach. This term, our Back to School initiative reached children across underserved communities with the supplies and support they needed to stay enrolled.

Beyond materials, our team worked directly with families and teachers to remove the barriers that push at-risk children out of the classroom, pairing each child with the encouragement and follow-up that keeps them learning.

Every child who stays in school is a future transformed. Thank you to everyone who made this possible.
