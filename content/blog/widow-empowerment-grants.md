When a woman loses her partner, she often loses her family's only source of income overnight. Our Women & Widow Empowerment programme exists to change that story — replacing dependency with dignity.

Through this cohort, participating widows received seed grants, financial literacy training and hands-on business mentorship. Many have already launched small trades and cooperatives, providing for their children with renewed confidence.

Empower a woman, and you empower an entire community. This is the work we are proud to keep doing.
