When a woman is empowered, an entire community rises with her. And when a widow loses her partner, she too often loses her family's only source of income overnight — left to raise children with little support and few options. Our Women & Widow Empowerment pillar is built to change that story, replacing dependency with dignity.

## What we do

- **Seed grants.** Start-up capital that helps women launch or grow a small business.
- **Business & financial literacy.** Practical training in trade skills, bookkeeping, savings and managing a small enterprise.
- **Cooperatives & support networks.** We connect women into peer groups that pool resources, share knowledge and stand with one another.
- **Mentorship.** Ongoing guidance so early wins turn into lasting, self-sufficient livelihoods.

## Our approach

We treat every woman as a capable entrepreneur, not a charity case. By combining capital with skills, community and mentorship, we help women move from surviving to thriving — providing for their children with renewed confidence and pride.

> Empower a woman, and you empower a family, a street, a whole community.

## How you can help

Fund a widow's seed grant, sponsor a training cohort, or lend your business expertise as a mentor. Your support helps a woman rebuild her life on her own terms.
