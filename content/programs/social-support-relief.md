Empowerment begins with meeting people where they are. Before a family can plan for tomorrow, they need to get through today — with food on the table, basic care within reach, and the knowledge that someone stands with them. Our Social Support & Relief pillar answers that immediate need with compassion and dignity.

## What we do

- **Food distribution.** Regular drives that deliver food items to families facing hardship.
- **Healthcare outreach.** Basic health checks and care taken directly into underserved and remote communities.
- **Emergency assistance.** Rapid support for families hit by crisis, loss or sudden need.
- **Warm referral.** We connect the people we help into our longer-term empowerment programmes, so relief becomes a first step, not a dead end.

## Our approach

Relief at Cheriven is never about handouts that create dependence. We provide urgent care with respect, and we use every point of contact as a bridge — from meeting an immediate need to opening a door toward lasting self-reliance.

> Compassion is where empowerment starts. We meet the need in front of us, then walk with people toward a stronger future.

## How you can help

Donate food or funds, join an outreach day as a volunteer, or help us respond faster to families in crisis. Your kindness meets a real need, right now.
