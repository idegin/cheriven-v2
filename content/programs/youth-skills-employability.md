Nigeria's greatest resource is its young people — bright, ambitious and full of potential. What too many of them lack is not talent, but opportunity. Our Youth Skills & Employability pillar bridges that gap, turning raw potential into real, market-relevant skills and a genuine path to self-reliance.

## What we do

- **Certified vocational training.** Hands-on skills in trades and services that are in demand in the local economy.
- **Digital & entrepreneurship skills.** Training that prepares young people for the modern workplace and for building their own ventures.
- **Start-up resources.** Toolkits and seed support that help graduates take their first concrete steps.
- **Mentorship.** Experienced mentors who guide young people through the transition from training to earning.

## Our approach

We design every cohort around the question, "What will actually help this young person earn a living?" That means market-relevant curricula, practical experience, and follow-through long after graduation — so a certificate becomes a career, not just a piece of paper.

> From skills to self-reliance — that is how lasting change is built, one young person at a time.

## How you can help

Sponsor a young person's training, donate tools and equipment, or offer mentorship and job opportunities. Together we can turn a generation's potential into progress.
