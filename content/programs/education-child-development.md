Every child deserves the chance to learn. Yet for too many Nigerian children — especially those from widow-headed and low-income homes — the cost of school fees, uniforms and books quietly forces them out of the classroom and into a cycle of poverty. Our Education & Child Development pillar exists to break that cycle, one child at a time.

## What we do

- **Scholarships & fees.** We cover tuition and examination costs so that money is never the reason a child stops learning.
- **Learning materials.** Books, uniforms, bags and stationery are provided through our back-to-school drives at the start of each term.
- **Mentorship & follow-up.** Every supported child is paired with encouragement and regular check-ins with families and teachers to keep them engaged and on track.

## Our approach

We don't just hand out supplies and walk away. We work in partnership with families and schools to remove the specific barriers pushing each child out of the classroom — from transport to nutrition to confidence — and we track progress so support translates into real, sustained learning.

> Educate a child today and you change a family's story for a generation.

## How you can help

Sponsor a child's education for a term, donate school materials, or volunteer as a mentor. Every contribution keeps a vulnerable child in school and full of hope.
