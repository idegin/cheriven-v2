A day set aside to honour, encourage and equip widows in our community. Widows Support & Grant Day is both a celebration and a launchpad — a chance for women to be seen, to connect with one another, and to receive the practical support they need to build independent livelihoods.

## What the day includes

- **Micro-grants** to provide start-up capital for small businesses.
- **Business mentorship** and short, practical training sessions.
- **A community network** — connecting widows into peer support groups that continue long after the event.
- **Encouragement and celebration** of the strength and resilience of the women we serve.

## Why we do this

Widows are among the most vulnerable in our society, yet also among the most determined. With the right support, they don't just recover — they thrive, and they lift their families with them.
