A practical, hands-on workshop designed to put in-demand skills within reach of youth and women who are ready to build a livelihood. Places are free but limited, and priority goes to participants from vulnerable and underserved homes.

## What you'll gain

- **Hands-on training** in market-relevant trades led by certified instructors.
- **Entrepreneurship basics** — finding customers, pricing and managing money.
- **A starter toolkit** to help you begin trading right away.
- **A mentor** to guide you through the crucial first months after the workshop.

## Who it's for

Young people and women looking for a genuine path from skills to self-reliance. No prior experience is required — just commitment and a willingness to learn.
