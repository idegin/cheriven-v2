Join Cheriven Empowerment Foundation for a full day of practical support and encouragement for vulnerable families in our host community. This outreach brings together several of our pillars in one place, so that people can meet an immediate need and take a first step toward lasting change on the same day.

## What to expect

- **Food distribution** for families facing hardship.
- **Basic health checks** and referrals delivered by our outreach team.
- **Empowerment counselling** — one-on-one conversations about education support, skills training and small-business opportunities.
- **Programme sign-ups** connecting attendees to our longer-term empowerment work.

## Who should come

Families in and around New Garki are warmly welcome. We especially encourage widows, women, youth and caregivers of persons with special needs to attend.

## Volunteer with us

We need extra hands to make the day run smoothly — from registration to distribution to counselling support. If you'd like to serve, [get in touch](/contact) or [sign up to volunteer](/volunteers).
