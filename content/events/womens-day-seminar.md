To mark International Women's Day, Cheriven Empowerment Foundation convened a seminar on **marriage, motherhood and emotional strength** — a gathering built on a simple conviction: when a woman is strengthened, a family is stabilised.

Led by our founder and convener, **Eucheria Ifeoma Ekweozoh**, the seminar brought together experts and women from across the community for honest conversations about resilience, mental wellbeing and the realities of family life.

## What the speakers shared

- **Eucheria Ifeoma Ekweozoh** — *Strength Beyond the Moment*: drawing strength and perspective through difficult seasons.
- **Barr. Mrs Ijeoma Okoro** — *Marriage, Resilience and Wisdom in Adversity*: navigating challenges with patience and wisdom.
- **Blessing Amaka Onwualu** — *Practical Motherhood, Endurance and Family Balance*: the everyday endurance of raising a family.
- **Jacqueline Ikeotuonye** (medical expert) — the emotional health of women as the foundation of a healthy home.

## Key messages

- Patience and emotional discipline are the quiet strength behind every stable home.
- Emotional exhaustion is not failure — and seeking help is a sign of strength, not weakness.
- Children need stability far more than they need parental perfection.
- A mother's mental wellbeing is the foundation of her family's health.
