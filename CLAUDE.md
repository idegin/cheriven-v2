# About the NGO
Cheriven Empowerment Foundation (CEF) is a legally registered Nigerian non-profit, non-political, and non-religious organization, duly incorporated as a CAC-registered Incorporated Trustees entity (IT-145738), committed to empowering vulnerable individuals and communities through structured and sustainable interventions.

## Mission
To design, implement, and scale inclusive empowerment programmes that improve access to education, vocational skills, sustainable livelihoods, and social support for underserved and at-risk populations across Nigeria.

## Vision
A Nigerian society where vulnerable individuals - including widows, women, youth, and children - are empowered to live with dignity, opportunity, and hope.
